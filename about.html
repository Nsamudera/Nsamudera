What is A Blank Canvas (ABC)

ABC is portfolio to showcase ideas, works, and projects as I start my journey in programming.

Where does the name A Blank Canvas comes from?

The name A Blank Canvas comes from the idiom with the same name.
In the idiom, blank canvas refers to something that has no substance or content imprinted on it.
This means that it has the capacity to be filled with infinite possibilities.
